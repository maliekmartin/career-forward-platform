## What Career Forward Is

Career Forward is a **workforce development platform** designed to help job seekers land their dream jobs faster while providing workforce organizations (American Job Centers, nonprofits, community colleges, etc.) with tools to track client progress and outcomes.

### The Problem We Solve

**For Job Seekers:**
- Job searching is overwhelming and disorganized
- Tracking applications across multiple platforms is chaotic
- Many lack access to professional resume-building tools
- Interview preparation resources are scattered and expensive
- No central place to manage the entire job search journey

**For Workforce Organizations:**
- Career coaches have limited visibility into what clients do between sessions
- Tracking outcomes for grant reporting (WIOA compliance) is manual and tedious
- No way to provide real-time feedback on resumes and applications
- Difficult to identify which clients need immediate support

### Our Solution

A unified platform that gives job seekers everything they need to succeed—resume builder, job tracker, interview prep, AI career coach—while giving workforce partners visibility into client progress (with consent).

## Key Features

### For Job Seekers (Free Forever)

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Resume Builder** | ATS-optimized templates designed by HR professionals. Create up to 3 versions. 100-point scoring system. Easy export to PDF. |
| **Job Tracker** | Kanban-style board to track every application. Stoplight status system (Green/Yellow/Red). Never lose track of where you applied. |
| **Interview Prep** | Video workshops, practice questions, mock interview techniques. Walk into every interview confident. |
| **AI Career Coach (Compass)** | 24/7 personalized guidance. Resume feedback, interview tips, job search strategies. Powered by AI but feels human. |
| **Career Assessments** | Discover strengths, interests, and matching career paths. |
| **Smart Reminders** | Automated follow-up reminders and application deadline alerts. |
| **Job Board Integration** | Aggregated listings from WorkSourceWA, USAJobs, JSearch API. |
| **Achievement System** | Badges and milestones to celebrate progress and stay motivated. |

### For Workforce Organizations (Paid)

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Caseload Management** | See all clients in one dashboard. Track engagement and progress at a glance. |
| **Outcome Tracking** | Track placements, interview rates, and success metrics. Generate reports for stakeholders. |
| **Exit Data & Reporting** | Capture exit wages, job titles, hours worked. WIOA-compliant reporting. |
| **Privacy Controls** | Job seekers control what coaches can see. Consent-based data sharing builds trust. |
| **In-App Messaging** | Communicate directly with clients. Full transparency protects coaches. |
| **Measurable Skill Gains** | Track training completion and skill development for compliance reporting. |

### Coming Soon
- **Learning Management System (LMS):** Assign training courses, track completion, measure skill gains
- **Mobile Apps:** iOS and Android (currently in development)
